🧠 AffectX

Emotion Intelligence Web Application

Comprehensive Project Report

Executive Summary

AffectX is an advanced emotion intelligence web application that leverages artificial intelligence and machine learning to detect, analyze, and provide insights into human emotions across multiple modalities (facial expressions, voice tone, and text sentiment). The application combines cutting-edge computer vision, audio processing, and NLP techniques to deliver comprehensive emotional wellness monitoring and support.

Problem Statement

Current Challenges

  1. Lack of Holistic Emotion Detection - Traditional applications focus on single emotion detection methods (face OR voice OR text), missing the complete emotional picture.
  2. Limited Emotional Intelligence - Users lack tools to understand their emotional patterns, triggers, and trends over time.
  3. Absence of AI-Powered Support - No intelligent systems to provide personalized emotional wellness guidance based on detected emotions.
  4. Poor UX in Emotion Analysis - Existing solutions have cluttered interfaces and don't present emotion data in digestible, actionable formats.
  5. Data Persistence Gap - Users cannot track their emotional journey or access historical emotional data.
  6. Bias in Detection Models - Face detection models tend to default to "neutral" emotion, making it difficult to detect negative emotions like sadness, anger, and disgust.

Solution Overview

AffectX addresses these challenges by providing a comprehensive, multi-modal emotion intelligence platform:

  • Multi-Modal Emotion Detection: Analyzes emotions from face, voice, and text inputs simultaneously
  • AI-Powered Analytics: Uses machine learning to generate insights, forecasts, and emotional intelligence metrics
  • Emotion-Aware Chatbot: Provides personalized emotional support conversations
  • Comprehensive Dashboard: Visual analytics with wellness scoring and emotional patterns
  • Persistent Storage: MongoDB-backed emotion history tracking
  • Professional UI/UX: Consistent, beautiful interface across all modules

Key Features

1. 👁️ Face Detection Module

  • Real-time Facial Expression Analysis: Detects 7 emotions (happy, sad, angry, surprised, fearful, disgusted, neutral)
  • Confidence Scoring: Shows confidence level for detected emotions
  • Emotion Capture: One-click capture and save of detected emotion
  • Live Webcam Feed: Real-time visualization with smooth performance
  • Bias Correction: Improved detection accuracy for non-neutral emotions

  • History Management: Stores and displays past text analyses

4. 🎯 Emotion Fusion Module

  • Cross-Modal Analysis: Combines face, voice, and text emotion data

5. 📊 Advanced Analytics Dashboard

  • Pattern Detection: Identifies recurring emotional patterns
  • AI-Generated Insights: Machine learning-powered recommendations

6. 💬 Emotion-Aware Chatbot

  • Emotion-Specific Responses: Tailored conversation based on detected emotion
  • Quick Reply Options: Pre-defined supportive messages
  • Coping Strategies: Time-based wellness recommendations

  • Conversational Support: Natural language understanding for user queries

7. 🔐 Authentication & Security

  • Secure Login: JWT-based authentication

  • Protected Routes: Route guards for authenticated pages
  • User Profiles: Personal user data management

Emotion Detection Algorithms

Face Emotion Detection

Uses pre-trained face-api.js model with facial landmark detection to classify expressions into 7 emotion categories with confidence scoring.

Voice Emotion Detection

Acoustic Features Extracted:

  • Spectral Centroid: Measures brightness/harshness of sound
  • Spectral Roll-off: Frequency distribution information
  • Energy (RMS): Overall loudness level
  • Chroma Features: Pitch and harmonic content

Emotion Scoring Logic:

Emotion Key Characteristics Acoustic Profile
Happy Pleasant, uplifting High energy + Bright tone + Stable rhythm
Sad Muted, withdrawn Low energy + Dark tone + Monotone
Angry Aggressive, harsh High energy + Harsh noise + Unstable rhythm
Surprised Sudden, variable Moderate energy + Bright + Variable pitch
Fearful Nervous, erratic High noise + Unstable + Erratic
Disgusted Dismissive, nasal Nasal quality + Dark tone + Low energy
Neutral Flat, controlled Moderate + Stable + Low variance

Dashboard Metrics & Analytics

Wellness Score (0-100)

Formula: 50 + (positive_ratio - negative_ratio) × 50 + confidence_boost
  • 0-20: Low emotional well-being
  • 21-40: Below average wellness
  • 41-60: Moderate wellness
  • 61-80: Good emotional health
  • 81-100: Excellent emotional well-being

Emotional Intelligence Score

Measures awareness of own emotions and ability to manage them. Based on emotion consistency, variety, and response patterns. Higher scores indicate better emotion regulation.

Emotional Stability Index

Measures consistency of emotions over time. Lower variance indicates higher stability and emotional resilience.

Mood Forecast

Predicts next 3-5 days of emotional trends based on historical patterns using machine learning algorithms. Helps with proactive emotional management.
